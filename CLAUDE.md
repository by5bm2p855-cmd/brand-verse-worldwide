## Chatbot — Verse

- Floating gold pill button, bottom-right corner of every page
- 11-step scripted intake flow (no AI API — guided questionnaire)
- Collects: business name, industry, budget, pages, style, colors, name, email, phone, notes
- Submits to Formspree → Ty gets an email brief with subject "New Client — [Business] (Budget)"
- "I have a question" branch: collects question + email, emails Ty, Formspree reply-to set to client email
- On success: button turns green, says "Request Sent ✓"
- Known fix: apostrophes in choice strings must use double quotes to avoid syntax errors
