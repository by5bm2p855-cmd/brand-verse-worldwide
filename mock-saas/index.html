What it does

Built for how
teams actually work.

01

Universal Connector

One integration unlocks Notion, Confluence, Slack, Google Drive, GitHub, Jira, and Linear simultaneously. Your knowledge lives in those tools — Nexus just makes it findable.

02

Semantic Search

Forget keywords. Ask questions the way you'd ask a colleague. Nexus understands context, synonyms, and intent — returning the right answer, not a list of documents.

03

Source Citations

Every answer includes a verifiable source link. Nexus never hallucinates — it only surfaces knowledge that already exists in your connected tools.

04

Permission Aware

Users only see answers built from content they already have access to. Nexus respects your existing access controls — zero configuration required.

05

Knowledge Graph

Watch your team's knowledge form into a living constellation. See which documents are connected, which are outdated, and what's actually being used.

06

Instant Onboarding

New team members get up to speed in hours, not weeks. Ask Nexus how the codebase works, who owns what, or why a decision was made in 2021.
