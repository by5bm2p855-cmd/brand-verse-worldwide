Beauty With Intention.

Lumière Studio was built on one idea — that every person who walks through our door deserves to leave feeling better than when they came in. Not just looking better. Actually feeling it.

We take our time. We listen. We use products that are good for your skin and your hair, not just products that sell. And we create an environment where you can actually exhale for a little while.
